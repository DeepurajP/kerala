Kerala Tourist Places


Located in the southwestern part, Kerala is an exquisite land in India. The unique landscape is bordered by the Western Ghats on one side and the Arabian Sea on the other. Thus it is blessed with awe-inspiring hill stations, exotic beaches, and lush greenery. The quiet backwaters and magnificent rivers accentuate the virgin beauty of the land. Kerala’s tourism industry is successful in showcasing the varied ecosystem of the state without affecting the natural balance. These initiatives secured Kerala a place in the list of Ten Paradises of the World by National Geographic Traveler Magazine

The tourism industry in Kerala strives for ecologically sustained tourism with sightseeing and adventure activities projecting the cultural and historical peculiarities of the land. Because of the diversity of the land, there are many tourist places worth a visit in Kerala. Tourists face difficulty deciding on how to use their time effectively while on Kerala tour packages. Here we list out some of the best places to visit in Kerala district-wise so that you will face no difficulty while preparing the itinerary for the land.

The beauty of Kerala is that there are still so many offbeat places that are being discovered in Kerala and the Kerala government is taking all the initiatives to protect, enhance, and beautify the state in the best possible manner. In fact, the tourism industry is focused on protecting the ecological balance among wildlife, climatic fluctuations, and even plant life. Kerala, if you didn’t know, is India’s traditional Ayurvedic study and preparation center. For those who love to visit tourist places in Kerala, we often advise you to experience the holistic treatments that traditional Ayurveda practitioners offer to tourists. The process is more wholesome than the ordinary spa treatments that you get in Western countries. Enjoy a blend of oils soaked with herbs that will be the everlasting cure for many skin diseases and internal body issues. It is a great remedy for a lot of physical and mental issues.
